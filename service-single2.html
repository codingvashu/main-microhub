What's included in CRM ?

Capture, organize, and manage leads and customer contacts in one centralized CRM system. Maintain complete records of customer details, communication history, and lead status.
Track leads through customizable sales stages and monitor deal progress in real time. Gain clarity on opportunities, expected revenue, and sales performance.
Never miss a follow-up with automated reminders and activity scheduling. Ensure timely communication with leads and customers to improve conversion rates.
Analyze lead sources, sales performance, and team activity using real-time CRM reports. Make informed decisions based on accurate customer and sales data.
Control access to CRM data with role-based permissions for sales teams, managers, and administrators. Ensure data security while enabling team collaboration.
